# Criminal Record Check

A certified document from your home country (and any other country where you have lived for an extended period) confirming you have no criminal record.

## What you need
- Official criminal record certificate (police clearance certificate) from your **home country**
- If you have lived in other countries for 1+ year, similar certificates may be required from those countries
- Must typically be issued within the past **3–6 months**
- Official translation into Japanese may be required

## Where to obtain
- **United States**: FBI Identity History Summary (request online via FBI website)
- **United Kingdom**: ACRO Criminal Records Office
- **Other countries**: Contact your country's national police authority or the relevant government ministry

## Tips
- Ordering this document takes time — some countries take 4–8 weeks. Start early.
- If a translation is required, use a **certified translator** (not machine translation).
- Any criminal record does not automatically disqualify you, but must be disclosed. Hiding it is grounds for permanent denial and possible deportation.
