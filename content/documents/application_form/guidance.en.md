# Permanent Residence Application Form (永住許可申請書)

The official application form for permanent residence, filed with the Immigration Services Agency.

## What you need
- Form available from the Immigration Services Agency (download or pick up at the immigration office)
- Complete in **Japanese** (or with Japanese translation)
- Attach one passport-sized photo

## Where to obtain
Download from the **Immigration Services Agency website** or pick up at your regional immigration office. There is currently no online submission — it must be submitted in person or by mail.

## Tips
- Fill in every field completely. Incomplete forms will be returned.
- Double-check that your name, address, and personal details are **identical** to your residence card and other submitted documents.
- After submitting, you will receive a receipt. Processing typically takes **4–6 months** — do not travel outside Japan in a way that would disrupt your residence continuity during this period.
- You may continue to work and live normally while the application is under review.
