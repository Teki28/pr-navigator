# Income Certificate (所得証明書)

An official certificate issued by your municipality showing your declared annual income, used to confirm financial stability.

## What you need
- Covering the past **3 years** is typically recommended
- Issued within the past **3 months**

## Where to obtain
Your **ward or municipal office (市区町村役場)**. Available at the counter or via convenience store terminal with a My Number Card.

## Tips
- The PR assessment considers a stable income. While there is no official minimum, a consistent income of **3 million JPY or more** per year is generally viewed favorably.
- If your income was lower in certain years (e.g., due to job change), consider including a brief letter of explanation.
- This document differs from the employer-issued "salary certificate (給与証明書)" — ensure you obtain the **municipal** income certificate.
