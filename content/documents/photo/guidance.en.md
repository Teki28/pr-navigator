# Application Photos (証明写真)

Passport-sized photographs are required for the PR application form.

## Specifications
- Size: **4 cm × 3 cm**
- Taken within the past **3 months**
- Plain white or light-colored background
- Full face, front-facing, no hat or sunglasses
- Name written on the back in pencil

## Where to obtain
Any **photo studio (証明写真スタジオ)** or photo booth (スピード写真) found in most train stations and convenience stores. Studio photos are recommended for the best quality.

## Tips
- Bring at least **2 copies** (one for the application form, one spare).
- Digital photos from photo booths may not meet the print quality standard — verify requirements.
