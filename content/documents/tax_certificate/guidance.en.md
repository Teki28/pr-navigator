# Tax Payment Certificate (納税証明書)

This document proves that you have paid your income tax obligations in Japan, demonstrating financial reliability.

## What you need
- Certificates covering the past **3–5 years** (immigration typically requests 3 years minimum)
- Both **income tax (所得税)** and **inhabitant tax (住民税)** certificates may be requested

## Where to obtain
- **Income tax certificate**: National Tax Agency office (税務署)
- **Inhabitant tax certificate**: Municipal office (市区町村役場)

Certificates can be requested in person or by mail. Allow a few days for processing.

## Tips
- If you have ever had outstanding tax payments, resolve them before applying. Any unpaid taxes are a significant negative factor in PR assessments.
- Keep receipts of all tax payments as supporting evidence.
