# HSP Point Calculation Sheet (高度専門職ポイント計算書)

This document demonstrates your eligibility under Japan's Highly Skilled Professional (高度専門職) points-based system and is essential for HSP-track PR applications.

## What you need
- Completed **point calculation table** using the official Ministry of Justice form
- Supporting documents for each claimed point (degree certificates, salary slips, academic papers, patents, etc.)

## Where to obtain
Download the official **Point Calculation Table** from the Immigration Services Agency website. Complete it yourself and attach supporting documents for every point claimed.

## Tips
- Points are awarded in three categories: **Academic background**, **Professional career**, and **Annual salary** — plus bonus points for Japanese language ability, study in Japan, innovative business activities, etc.
- **80 points or more** → eligible after 1 year of residence.
- **70 points or more** → eligible after 3 years of residence.
- Only count points that you can **document**. Overstating points is grounds for rejection.
- If you previously held HSP visa status, include your visa status history documents.
