# Residence Card (在留カード)

Your residence card is your primary proof of legal residence status in Japan, issued by the Immigration Services Agency.

## What you need
- Current valid residence card
- Photocopy of both front and back sides

## Where to obtain
Your residence card is issued automatically when your visa status is granted or renewed at a **regional immigration office**. Replacement cards can be obtained there if yours is lost or damaged.

## Tips
- Ensure your **registered address** on the card is current — it must match your resident record.
- If your card will expire before your application is processed, renew it first.
- Immigration may request previous residence cards to document your full residence history.
