# Employment Certificate (在職証明書)

An official letter from your employer confirming your current employment status, job title, and salary, proving stable employment in Japan.

## What you need
- Official company letterhead with employer's seal (会社印)
- Must include: your full name, position, employment start date, annual salary, and employment type (full-time, etc.)

## Where to obtain
Request it from your company's **HR department**. There is no official template; your company will issue it according to their standard format.

## Tips
- Allow at least **1–2 weeks** for your HR department to prepare and issue this document.
- If you are self-employed, substitute this with your **business registration certificate** and recent financial statements.
- Ensure the document is dated within the past **3 months** at the time of application submission.
