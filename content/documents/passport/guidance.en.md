# Passport

Your current valid passport is required to verify your identity and nationality.

## What you need
- Must be valid for the entire PR application period (aim for 6+ months remaining validity)
- Provide photocopies of **all pages** that contain personal information, entry/exit stamps, and visas

## Where to obtain
Contact your **home country's embassy or consulate** in Japan if your passport has expired or will expire soon. Renewal timelines vary by nationality — start early.

## Tips
- If you have held multiple passports (old and new), bring both; immigration may request earlier passports to verify your continuous residence history.
- Make sure your name matches exactly across all submitted documents.
