# Resident Record (住民票)

The resident record (jūminhyō) is an official document issued by your municipality confirming your registered address and household members in Japan.

## What you need
- Full copy including all household members
- Issued within the past **3 months**
- Must include your **My Number** (individual number) if requested

## Where to obtain
Visit the **ward or municipal office (市区町村役場)** where you are registered. You can also use the convenience store terminal (コンビニ交付) if registered with My Number Card.

## Tips
- Bring your **residence card and My Number card** for identification.
- If you have recently moved, ensure you have updated your address at the local municipal office within 14 days of moving (legally required).
